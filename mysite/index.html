BCA Course Description

Jan 23, 2023

A Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate degree course in the field of computer applications. It typically lasts for four years and covers topics such as programming, data structures, database management, computer networks, web development, and software engineering. The course is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and prepare them for a career in the IT industry even they are from non-science background. Some universities also offer a BCA program with a specialization in a particular area, such as software development or networking. Graduates of the BCA course can pursue a career as a software developer, computer programmer, system administrator, or in any other IT-related field.

BCA Course In Nepal.

Jan 16, 2023

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is a four-year undergraduate course offered by various universities in Nepal. It is a program designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of computer science, programming, and application development. The course curriculum covers a wide range of subjects such as programming languages, data structures, database management, computer networks, web development, and software engineering. Students are also exposed to various practical applications of computer science, such as artificial intelligence, data analytics, and cloud computing. The BCA course in Nepal is designed to meet the growing demand for computer professionals in the country. Graduates of the program can pursue careers in various fields such as software development, web development, IT consulting, and data analysis. The course is open to students who have completed their 10+2 or equivalent education in any stream. However, some universities may have specific eligibility criteria or entrance exams that students must pass in order to be admitted to the program. Overall, the BCA course in Nepal is a great option for students who are interested in computer science and want to pursue a career in the field. It provides them with a solid foundation in the subject and prepares them for a wide range of professional opportunities. Colleges in Nepal that offer BCA are under the affiliation of Tribhuvan University, Purbanchal University and Pokhara University.
Eligibility:
The students will need minimum D (D+ in Pokhara University) grade in each subject of grade 11 and 12 with CGPA 2.0 or more, minimum score of second division marks in 10+2, PCL or equivalent in any discipline. Students who have passed grade 11 and are waiting for supplementary exam (PURAK PARIKSHA) of grade 12 can also apply. However, they have to submit all the required documents at the time of admission.

BCA Course In Pokhara University.

Jan 16, 2023

According to Pokhara University, "Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA), an undergraduate course, is a unique blend of Information Technology (IT) with business and management. In this dynamic technological era, fast growing information technology and communication systems require expert professionals, who can apply computer science principles to solve business and technology related problems. In recent years, BCA has emerged as one of the most demanding technical courses. This course transforms students into high caliber IT professionals most sought in any organization and IT industry."
